## Context

Matt is building **PRVT MKT** (`prvmkt.ai`) — a verticalized AI automation and bespoke software shop targeting CRE (Commercial Real Estate) Private Equity firms.

### Mission
Protect and grow NOI by automating the industry's most tedious manual processes.

### Core Products (Standalone Automations)

1. **Fundraising & LP Outreach Velocity** — Automates top-of-funnel for capital raises. LP vetting from databases + hyper-personalized outreach + HubSpot CRM integration. Scales "founder's touch" without the manual grind.

2. **Document Abstraction Engine** — Ingests 50+ page PDFs (leases, loan docs, JV agreements, operating agreements, REAs, title, environmental). Outputs structured "Data Artifacts" with key dates, obligations, risk clauses. Converts hours of associate labor into seconds.

3. **Investor Reporting Narrative Automation** — Transforms raw property data (NOI, occupancy, capex) into professional, brand-aligned quarterly reports that "read like the GP." Eliminates the Quarterly Crunch.

4. **CAM Reconciliation (The Leakage Hunter)** — Audits GL expenses against lease recovery clauses. Flags unrecovered expenses, auto-generates reconciliation statements. Direct NOI growth by recovering "leaked" revenue.

5. **Personalized Dashboard / Command Center** — Single-pane-of-glass for executives. Surfaces only critical actions: leases to sign, budgets to review, priority LP follow-ups.

6. **Advisory** — Software advisory, AI strategy, team adoption.
